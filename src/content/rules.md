# Tournament Rules & Policies

> Please review all policies and procedures. If you have additional questions, please contact us directly at [info@volleymonster.com](mailto:info@volleymonster.com).

## What to Bring

- Water will be available, but bringing your own is recommended.
- Chairs and a canopy for shade are encouraged.

## Park Policies

- Dogs must be leashed at all times.
- Alcohol, cigarettes, and e-cigs are prohibited. No open or visible containers allowed.

## Refund Policy

- Withdrawals before 5:00 PM, 2 days prior to the tournament, receive a full refund.
- After this time, refunds will be issued as credit for future entry or merch.
- No refunds once check-in has ended for your event.
- Any players who are banned from tournament entry are not eligible for refund under any circumstances.

## Weather Policy

- Play continues in light rain but pauses for thunderstorms.
- If the event is canceled due to severe weather and cannot be rescheduled, a full refund will be issued.
